Lasagna is a popular Italian dish made of stacked layers of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, and creamy cheese. It is often baked in the oven, allowing the flavors to blend together perfectly. The most common version includes ground beef, tomato sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella cheese. However, many variations exist, such as vegetarian lasagna with spinach or zucchini.

Ingredients used

  • ground beef
  • Italian sausage
  • ricotta cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • various herbs and spices
